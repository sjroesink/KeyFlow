Moonlight Sonata

Ludwig van Beethoven

Tempo Adagio
Key C# Minor
Duration 6:15

About the Piece

The Piano Sonata No. 14, popularly known as the Moonlight Sonata, is one of Beethoven's most enduring works. Completed in 1801, it broke traditional sonata form by beginning with a slow, hypnotic movement that feels more like a fantasy than a formal allegro.

For the student, this piece represents a mastery of emotional restraint and rhythmic precision within the famous triplets of the first movement.
